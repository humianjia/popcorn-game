Cookie Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2024

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile) when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences, understand how you use the site, and improve your overall experience.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

2.1 Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly. They enable core functionality such as:

  • Security features
  • Load balancing
  • Basic website navigation

Note: Essential cookies cannot be disabled as they are required for the website to work properly.

2.2 Performance Cookies

These cookies collect information about how visitors use our website, including:

  • Pages visited
  • Time spent on pages
  • Error messages received
  • Which links are clicked

This data is anonymized and used solely to improve our website's functionality.

2.3 Functionality Cookies

These cookies allow our website to remember your preferences and choices, including:

  • Language preferences
  • Game settings
  • Login information (if applicable)

2.4 Advertising Cookies

Our website displays advertisements from third-party ad networks. These cookies may be used to:

  • Deliver relevant advertisements based on your interests
  • Track the effectiveness of ads
  • Remember your browsing habits

Third-party advertisers may also use cookies to build a profile of your interests and show you relevant ads on other websites.

2.5 Game-Specific Cookies

When you play games on our platform, additional cookies may be set to:

  • Save game progress
  • Store game settings
  • Track game statistics

3. Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies on our website are set by third-party services, including:

  • Google Analytics: For website traffic analysis
  • Google AdSense: For advertising delivery
  • Game Providers: Third-party games may set their own cookies

These third-party services have their own privacy and cookie policies. We encourage you to review the policies of these third parties.

4. How Long Do Cookies Last?

Cookies can be either:

  • Session Cookies: Deleted when you close your browser
  • Persistent Cookies: Remain on your device for a set period or until manually deleted

Persistent cookies typically last between 24 hours to 2 years, depending on the specific cookie.

5. How to Manage Cookies

You can control and manage cookies in several ways:

5.1 Browser Settings

Most browsers allow you to:

  • View what cookies are stored
  • Delete specific cookies
  • Block cookies from all or certain websites
  • Block third-party cookies
  • Clear all cookies when you close the browser

To learn how to manage cookies in your browser:

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Safari
  • Microsoft Edge

5.2 Opt-Out Links

You can opt out of certain advertising cookies using these links:

  • Google Ads Settings
  • Digital Advertising Alliance

5.3 Browser Extensions

Various browser extensions are available that can help manage cookies, such as privacy-focused ad blockers.

6. Impact of Disabling Cookies

If you choose to disable cookies, please note that:

  • Some website features may not work properly
  • Game progress may not be saved
  • Your preferences may not be remembered
  • You may see more irrelevant advertisements

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.

We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about our use of cookies.
